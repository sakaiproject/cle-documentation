Community

Sakai is a community of interest focused on developing applications that support teaching, learning, and research in the larger context of the Apereo Foundation.  This community includes educational institutions (primary, secondary, and gradulate), commercial affiliates, and other organizations interested promoting and advancing the goals of the community.
